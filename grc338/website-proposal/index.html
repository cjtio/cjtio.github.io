Introduction

The Craft Barbershop

The Craft is a barbershop that specializes in creating a classic, laid-back experience for its patrons. Patrons can enjoy a glass of beer or wine during their haircut and enjoy great conversation.

Target audience

The target audience would be adults who value quality haircuts.

Their primary tasks would likely be to schedule appointments with barbers and see how much it costs to get a haircut at The Craft. Another task as well would be to look for contact information and the location of the business.
